Potato cake

Home Gnocchi Milanesas

The best potato cake you will ever try!

This is a very easy and delicious dish that will take only a few ingredients to make. It's a dish that will allow you to add your personal signature! make it your own!

Ingredients:

  • 1kg potatoes
  • 100gr of butter
  • 200gr of cheese
  • 1kg of groung meat
  • 2 egg
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat the oven at 150 degrees celcius for about and hour
  2. Wash and peel the potatos
  3. Place them in a pot with water and sal and boil
  4. Once the potatoes are cooked, mash them trying to avoid lumps
  5. Add the butter and mash a bit more
  6. Put the ground meat in an oven friendly pot and add the egg and condimens
  7. Put the pot in the oven for about 30 mins
  8. Take the meatlof out and cover it with a lyer of mashed potatoes
  9. Put slices of chees on top of the potatoes and take to the oven until the cheese has gratinated
  10. Take out and ready to serve!
