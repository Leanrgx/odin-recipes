Potato cake

This is a very easy and delicious dish that will take only a few ingredients to make. It's a dish that will allow you to add your personal signature! make it your own!

Ingredients:

  • 1kg potatoes
  • 100gr of butter
  • 200gr of cheese
  • 1kg of ground meat
  • 2 egg
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat the oven at 150 degrees Celsius for about and hour
  2. Wash and peel the potatoes
  3. Place them in a pot with water and salt and boil
  4. Once the potatoes are cooked, mash them trying to avoid lumps
  5. Add the butter and mash a bit more
  6. Put the ground meat in an oven friendly pot and add the egg and condiments
  7. Put the pot in the oven for about 30 minutes
  8. Take the meatloaf out and cover it with a layer of mashed potatoes
  9. Put slices of cheese on top of the potatoes and take to the oven until the cheese has gratinate
  10. Take out and ready to serve!
