Gnocchi with mushroom sauce

The following is a very simple recipe to make the most delicious homemade gnocchi that you will every try. To go ahead with the recipe you will need the following ingredients:

Ingredients:

For the gnocchi
  • 1 kg potatoes
  • 500 gr flour
  • 1 egg
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
For the sauce
  • 250 gr Mushrooms
  • 1 medium-sized onion
  • 1 Green onion
  • 250 ml Milk cream
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps:

Gnocchi Dough
  1. Wash and peel the potatoes
  2. Place them in a pot with water and boil
  3. Once the potatoes are cooked mash them trying to avoid lumps
  4. Add the egg and the condiments
  5. Add the flour little by little, kneading the dough until the texture is consistent
  6. Set aside
Mushroom Sauce
  1. Wash mushrooms and onions
  2. Cut the mushrooms in thin slices and set aside
  3. Cut the onion in cubes and stir-fry until translucent
  4. Cut the green onion and separate the green part for later
  5. Add the mushrooms to the pan.
  6. After a few minutes add the milk cream
  7. Add salt and pepper
  8. Add the leaves of the green onions
Preparing the Gnocchi
  1. Put a pot with water to boil
  2. Spread some flour on a plate
  3. Take a dollop of dough and make balls of about the size of a small grape or a big blueberry (you can get some flour on your hands to avoid stickiness)
  4. Take a fork and run the ball of dough along its teeth in a quick motion. This should leave the imprint of your finger in the ball making it look like shell pasta
  5. Once you have made a few of them go ahead and put them in the boiling water. Make sure not to put too many at the same time.
  6. When the gnocchi start to float it's time to serve!
